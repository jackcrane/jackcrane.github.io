The internet is a confusing place

Clear it up with Educated

The world we live in is hugely divided by opposing political ideals and priorities. This tends to come over through our news outlets. Weather or not they are attempting to, most news sources have consistent political ideals which could be either conservative, moderate, or liberal.

Introducing educated

A Google Chrome extension that scans through the content being fed to you through the internet. As of now, Educated scans your Google search results and will add a notice to each with a recognizable color theme.
